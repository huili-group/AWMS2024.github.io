The Asian Workshop on Molecular Spectroscopy (AWMS) is committed to promoting academic exchanges between experimental and theoretical researchers worldwide in the field of molecular spectroscopy from all over the world, especially encouraging young scientists to present their latest work. The upcoming 9th AWMS is scheduled to take place at The Unversity of New South Wales,in Sydney，Australia on December 9 to December 12, 2025. This conference will feature 8 topics and host about 4 plenary speakers and 20 invited speakers, and about 150 participants. We look forward to your participatio

Theme

The themes that we intend to cover in this meeting are as follows:

  • Spectroscopy in gas phase
  • Application of spectroscopy to dynamics
  • New spectroscopic techniques
  • Precision measurement
  • Theoretical analysis
  • Spectroscopy of condensed phase/solid
  • AI for spectroscopy
  • Atmospheric and interstellar spectra
  • Other topics
