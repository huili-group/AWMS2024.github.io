8th AWMS

Introduction

Circular Lecture Hall, State Key Laboratory of Supramolecular Structure and Materials

The Asian Workshop on Molecular Spectroscopy (AWMS) is committed to promoting academic exchanges between experimental and theoretical researchers worldwide in the field of molecular spectroscopy from all over the world, especially encouraging young scientists to present their latest work. The upcoming 8th AWMS is scheduled to take place at Jilin University in Changchun , China on September 11 to September 14, 2024. This conference will feature 8 topics and host about 4 plenary speakers and 20 invited speakers, and about 150 participants. We look forward to your participation.

Theme

The themes that we intend to cover in this meeting are as follows:

  • Spectroscopy in gas phase
  • Application of spectroscopy to dynamics
  • New spectroscopic techniques
  • Precision measurement
  • Theoretical analysis
  • Spectroscopy of condensed phase/solid
  • AI for spectroscopy
  • Atmospheric and interstellar spectra
  • Other topics
